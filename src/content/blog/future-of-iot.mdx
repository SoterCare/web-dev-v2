# The Future of IoT in Healthcare

The Internet of Things (IoT) is transforming how we approach healthcare, particularly for the elderly. With devices like **SoterCare**, we can now monitor vital signs in real-time without invasive procedures.

## Key Benefits

1. **Continuous Monitoring**: 24/7 tracking of vitals.
2. **Instant Alerts**: Immediate notification of falls or health anomalies.
3. **Peace of Mind**: Families can rest easy knowing their loved ones are safe.

> "Technology is best when it brings people together."

## Integrating with AI

By combining IoT data with Machine Learning, we can predict health trends before they become emergencies.
